Usage Guide

Email Signature

  • Use hexprove-icon.svg (120x120px)
  • Resize to 60x60px or 80x80px for signatures
  • Place next to your name and title

Email Marketing

  • Use hexprove-logo-horizontal.svg in headers
  • Keep minimum width of 200px for readability
  • Ensure good contrast on background

Colors

  • Primary: #00d4aa (Teal)
  • Works best on dark backgrounds (#000, #0a0a0a, #1a1a1a)
  • For light backgrounds, consider adding a dark container
