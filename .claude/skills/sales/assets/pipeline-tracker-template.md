# HexProve Sales Pipeline Tracker

## Quick Setup Guide

Copy this structure into Notion or a spreadsheet to track your pipeline.

---

## Pipeline Board (Notion/Kanban Style)

### Columns:

**1. Lead Identified**
New leads that need research

**2. Researched**  
Full research complete, ready for outreach

**3. Outreach Sent**
First message sent, waiting for response

**4. Response Received**
They replied (positive or neutral)

**5. Discovery Scheduled**
Call is booked

**6. Discovery Complete**
Call happened, qualified

**7. Proposal Sent**
Proposal delivered

**8. Negotiation**
Discussing terms

**9. Closed Won** 🎉
Deal signed

**10. Closed Lost**
They passed (document why)

## Metrics to Track

### Activity Metrics (Weekly)
- Leads researched
- Outreach messages sent
- Follow-ups sent
- Discovery calls completed
- Proposals sent

### Conversion Metrics (Monthly)
- Lead → Researched: X%
- Outreach → Response: X%
- Response → Discovery: X%
- Discovery → Proposal: X%
- Proposal → Close: X%
- Overall Lead → Close: X%

### Outcome Metrics (Monthly)
- Deals won
- Deals lost
- Revenue closed
- Average deal size
- Sales cycle length (days)

### Pipeline Metrics (Weekly)
- Total pipeline value
- Pipeline by stage
- Deals aging (how long in each stage)
- Expected close this month

---

## Common Lost Reasons to Track

- No budget
- Went with competitor
- Decided to hire instead
- Timing not right
- No response (ghosted)
- Too early stage
- Not a fit (wrong product type)
- Internal QA sufficient
- Other: [specify]

Tracking lost reasons helps identify patterns and improve positioning.
